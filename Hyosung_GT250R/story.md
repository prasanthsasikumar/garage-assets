# Hyosung GT250R

The big boy. A V-twin in the 250cc class was a genuinely unusual proposition, and the GT250R made the most of it — more torque than the parallel-twin alternatives, a deeper exhaust note, and a road presence that punched well above its displacement. It looked serious and felt serious, the kind of bike that made every ride feel like a proper occasion.
