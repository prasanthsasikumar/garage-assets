# TGB Tapo

Sometimes you just need something to get from A to B without drama. The TGB Tapo was exactly that — practical, reliable, and perfectly suited to weaving through city traffic. It asked for very little and delivered everything it promised. Not every vehicle needs to be an adventure, and the Tapo was unapologetically good at being useful.
