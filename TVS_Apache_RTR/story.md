# TVS Apache RTR

My first bike. There is something about the machine that introduces you to motorcycling that stays with you permanently — and the Apache RTR was a solid place to start. It was reliable in the way that matters most when you are still learning, and had enough performance to feel genuinely exciting without being unforgiving. Quick, dependable, and the beginning of everything that followed.
