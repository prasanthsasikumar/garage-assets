# Yamaha R3

The R3 sits at a sweet spot that very few bikes manage to hit: genuinely fun without being intimidating, quick enough to keep things interesting, and forgiving enough to learn on properly. It wore its sportbike styling honestly — not a large bike dressed up with race bodywork, but a properly sorted machine that rewarded smooth riding and good technique. Great in the twisties and genuinely easy to live with every day.
