# Subaru Legacy (Humayun)

We found a very tidy 1999 Subaru Legacy Twin Turbo in manual — exactly the kind of car my friend Humayun had been after. He was already deeply into cars, and went on to own many Legacies and Porsches over the years, so it felt fitting to get him started properly.

The car ran an aftermarket exhaust that had been certified — loud enough to make its presence known everywhere, but entirely legal. Clean, characterful, and exactly what it needed to be.
